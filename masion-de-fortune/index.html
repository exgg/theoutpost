Maison-De-Fortune

The House Of Fortune

Introduction

Located on the edge of London's Regents Park, a grand townhouse once the residence of a lucky French Nobleman, is now home to an extraordinary group of individuals.

This is the story of the collective secretly known as the Maison De Fortune, a fellowship of spiritualists each one a specialist in the esoteric arts.

The house shrouded in an air of mystique, serves as a base where they live and work to transform their client's lives, by decoding incidents of spiritual significance. As these modern day fortunetellers venture further into their mystical craft, the puzzles they encounter become more intricate. With each cryptic revelation, the lives of their clients and their own become increasingly Intertwined.

Consumed by their mutal passion for art, fashion, jewellery the finer things in life, the group find themselves entangled in their complex problems, and relationships along with a progressively puzzling signal from another dimension.

The situation only grows more complicated as the journey unfolds, for the group, their clients, the humans and entities that surround them.

Are you ready to step inside, the Maison De Fortune?
