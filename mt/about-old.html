I have made it my goal to spread the knowledge I have gained in my clinical experience of treating anxiety as widely as possible.


"This stuff should be taught at school and be common knowledge everywhere”“ – Manuel Toren.


Manuel’s therapeutic aim is to unlock people’s full potential by eliminating anxiety. Common anxiety-related problems include:-

  • Insomnia - Excessive worry
  • Procrastination - Panic attacks
  • Avoidance - Repetitive behaviours
  • Inertia/stuckness - Repetitive checking

    • Manuel has worked on anxiety disorders in his NHS practice for years. He can treat the following anxiety disorders:

  • Panic disorder
  • Generalised anxiety disorder
  • Post-traumatic stress disorder
  • Social anxiety disorder
  • Phobias
  • Death anxiety
  • Health anxiety disorder
  • Obsessive-compulsive disorder
  • Body Dysmorphic Disorder
  • Sexual dysfunction

    • Neuro-Bridging is a therapy and wellness product based on the neuroscience of memory, working directly on client-specified issues. see more here
