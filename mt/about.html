About Manuel Toren As someone who himself suffered from anxiety for many years, Manuel Toren has made it his goal to spread the knowledge he gained in his own struggles to overcome anxiety as widely as possible. “This stuff should be taught at school and be common knowledge everywhere” – Manuel Toren. Manuel’s therapeutic aim is to unlock people’s full potential by eliminating anxiety. Common anxiety-related problems include:- - Insomnia - Excessive worry - Procrastination - Panic attacks - Avoidance - Repetitive behaviours - Inertia/stuckness - Repetitive checking Manuel has worked on anxiety disorders in his NHS practice for years.
