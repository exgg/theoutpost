The Best Barista In The World

A warm, chaotic, and bittersweet series about coffee, connection, and chasing a better life.

Episode One: Chiltern Street Macchiato

Naida and Rocco open the café. George teases, Naida chats nails, and a rock star makes a move. A strong macchiato to start.

Episode Two: Influencer Coffee

Pippa storms in needing content. Naida and Rocco get rattled when she shares a rival café's cake online. Chaos brews.

Episode Three: Flat White

Orders stack, regulars ramble, deliveries collide. Axel proposes an event, and Rocco gets a lead on a flat. Busy vibes.

Episode Four: Rules

Early customers, secret coffee out, and a fortune teller predicts Naida's fate. Property viewings and patience running low.

Episode Five: Beans

Tom delivers beans, Naida works the pub, and goes home sad. Coffee doesn’t ship itself. Life’s not where she wants it to be.

Episode Six: Complaints and Enquiries

Bad reviews surface. Axel wants to host his event, but has he read the comments? Tensions and smiles forced.

Episode Seven: I Miss My Boyfriend

Breakups and bench kisses. Naida and Rocco go extra polite, and a girl finally buys the mag after snapping it page by page.

Episode Eight: The Hotelier

A hotel drama, a violent maître d’, and Rocco’s flat falls through. Naida goes running, César comes crawling.

Episode Nine: Making Moves

Romance blooms. Rocco moves into the storeroom. An art exhibition is planned. Everyone helps with the beans.

Episode Ten: Date Night

Naida and Axel connect in a hotel suite. Rocco flirts, dodges George, and heads to bed among the sacks of beans. Life, bittersweet.
