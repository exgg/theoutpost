Book coming Spring 2025: The Story of Todd Hunter Antiques



In London's upscale Mayfair, American antique dealer Todd Hunter runs a salon gallery where he'd rather admire his collection than sell it.

But the art world's demands push him into an endless cycle of buying and selling, caught between his love for fine art and the need to survive. With his fiancé Nancy, a savvy dealer herself, Todd navigates the electric world of high-stakes collectors, secrets, and moral compromise.
