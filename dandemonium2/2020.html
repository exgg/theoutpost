THE eBOOK

2020

Journalist, author, and curator Robin Dutt returns with 2020, a contemplative collection of 20 short stories and 20 poems. This new volume explores everyday reflections, childhood memory, fable, and fantasy—each piece a mirror to the shared human experience.

With gentle observation and philosophical enquiry, Dutt reminds us how much of life is chosen—and how much chooses us. The tales, like the poems, offer a subtle kind of intimacy, revealing truths shaped by the brushstrokes of memory and imagination.

Profound yet playful, 2020 is an invitation to journey inward—whether through a remembered bedtime story or a fleeting moment on a city street.
