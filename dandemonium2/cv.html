ROBIN DUTT

JOURNALIST – STYLIST – ART CRITIC – CURATOR

20 / 20 Home Subscribe About Robin

PROFILE

Working in the worlds of art, fashion and lifestyle in a journalistic, public relations and event organising capacity.

  • Responsible for several ‘firsts’ in the art world including composite exhibitions such as I Criticus and The Kiss, established in 1993.
  • Created and executed public relations strategies for corporations and events.
  • TV Presenter for Lifestyle, Art and Fashion programmes across various channels.

MEDIA (1987 to date)

Contributions to Clothes Show Magazine, Esquire, The Times, The Independent, The Express, Elle, The London Magazine, Time Out, Attitude, and many others.

Interviews with fashion figures such as Pierre Cardin, Issey Miyake, Jean Paul Gaultier, Vivienne Westwood, and artists like Grayson Perry, Gilbert & George.

TV, RADIO AND ARCHIVE

Appearances on Granada TV, BBC World Service, Channel 4, BBC Radio, and Channel 5. Interviewer for British Library’s National Sound Archives.

PUBLIC RELATIONS

Consultancy for West Soho Association, Sister Management, Regent Street Association, and more.

STYLING / ART DIRECTION

Concept and launch execution for events and brands including Soho London & NY, Royal Auping, Vaseline Bodies Photography Show, and more.

ART

Founder of West Soho Gallery. Curated exhibitions including early Hussein Chalayan work and the ‘Kiss’ show. Advised on and authored catalogues for leading galleries.

PUBLICATIONS

Gilbert & George – Obsessions & Compulsions (2004), Martin Leman’s Cats (2005), Lovely Ladies (2005)

PHOTOGRAPHS, PAINTINGS & INTERVIEWS

Painted by leading artists; featured in Italian Vogue, Sunday Times, Independent Magazine, and multiple books on fashion and Dandyism.

FILM

Costume designer, actor and period consultant for Against Nature (2005), awarded Best Film and Director in LA.

MISCELLANEOUS

Founded Style and Arts Society at Home House London. Instructor of Fencing. Book Club and Creative Writing President.

LECTURING / EXPERIENCE (1990 to date)

Visiting Professor, AIU London. Guest lecturer at Royal College of Art, London Institute, West London College, and speaker at London Fashion Week and The Art Fund.
