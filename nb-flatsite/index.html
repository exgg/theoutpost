Neuro-Bridging

Turn Off The Stress Switch

With Neuro-Bridging, a digital therapeutic that enables individuals to eliminate post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) symptoms through a guided imagery exercise, rooted in the neuroscience of memory reconsolidation.

Who is it for?

Individuals with PTSD symptoms

For people experiencing intrusive thoughts, hyper-arousal, nightmares, avoidance, or persistent stress responses following trauma.

Those Affected by General Stress Conditions

This technique has the potential to help anyone struggling with stress, anxiety, cravings, compulsive behaviors, or persistent negative thinking.

How It Works

1 Measure 2 Learn 3 Practise 4 Follow up
1

Measure (Baseline)

Start with a brief assessment using standard clinical measures to understand your current state.

2

Learn the Technique

Access guided audio lessons or learn with our therapist, the app includes everything you need and guides you through each step of the process.

3

Practice (Sessions)

You'll complete a series of short sessions using the technique, each lasting 3 minutes, every half hour over the course of a day. The system will email you reminders to help you stay on track. Consistent practice is essential to achieving change.

4

Follow Up

Evaluate your progress by comparing your latest results with your initial measurement. The cycle can be repeated if required.

FAQ

1

Do I need headphones?

Optional but helpful. Headphones can improve focus during the audio steps, but phone or computer speakers work just as well. The program functions effectively without them.

2

How was Neuro-Bridging developed?

Neuro-Bridging was developed in the field by highly skilled therapists in real-world settings. The app and process were the result of their work over many years.

3

Do I need to use the app alongside a therapist?

At this stage, yes. Strong emotions may arise during the process, and as the product is still in development, we want you to be supported by a qualified therapist for your safety.

4

Is Neuro-Bridging just another wellness app?

Many wellness and meditation apps provide only temporary relief or, at best, incremental change. Neuro-Bridging delivers lasting, transformational results, symptoms are resolved, and users experience a new quality of life. Neuro-Bridging is a digital therapeutic, not a wellness app.
