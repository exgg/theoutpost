We are a boutique business consulting company based in London with an International approach. We can support companies and projects in different countries, with special focus on UK and Asia, thanks to the special background of the founder.
Our mission is to help you to achieve your goals using a very customized 360 degree approach according to yours and your clients’ specific needs to help you to succeed in the long term. Our values: we offer creative solutions which evolve according to the changes in the market and clients.

With over 13 years experience in luxury business, I have worked for multiple Brands like Max Mara, Luxottica and Armani group across Asia and UK. During my career I had the chance to be in charge of successful projects, including the set up of the retail foundations of Armani Brands in China. Languages: Italian, English, Mandarin.
