Business strategy and development


Business strategy and Intelligence

We help you to develop and plan the best short/long-term strategy to achieve your business goals. Working according to the business needs In the respective local market.

Specializing in the UK, Europe, China and the rest of Asia.

Collecting and processing data from various sources we provide insights into market trends, customer behaviour and operational efficiency.


Go to market

If you want to enter a new market we can support you throughout the entire process. From the initial stages related to the set-up of a company to the start of your business in a local market.


Project Management

From the launch of a pop-up, to a store opening/closure, or any project that a company might have, we ensure each phase is efficiently executed, on time and within budget. This service includes planning, organizing, coordinating and controlling tasks related to any retail/wholesale initiative.


Interim Management

We cover temporary executive or managerial positions at the corporate level as well as in-store. This allows the business to benefit quickly from our expertise and guidance. of a highly skilled professional.

We can intervene to provide a presence, starting from 1 day a week to full-time for periods from 1 to 12 months or longer.

The service is ideal for achieving defined objectives, stabilize operations, or steer a company through a transition until a permanent solution or a management team is established.

It’s the perfect solution for companies facing unexpected challenges or for instance lacking a local structure which requires specific skills for a finite period.

Some of the roles that we can cover include: Country Manager, Retail Director/Manager, Retail Project Manager, Business Developer, HR Manager, CRM, Retail Marketing Manager, Store Manager/Director.


Reorganization and recruiting

WWe help you to optimize and improve the efficiency of your corporate and in-store team structure. Once agreed on the best solution for your business, we proceed to support you during the local recruiting process, thanks also to our network of local recruiting experts.

We can also support you in recruiting and supervising teams during pop-up or other short-term projects.
