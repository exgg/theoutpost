Business strategy and development

Business strategy and Intelligence

We help you to develop and plan the best short term or long term strategy to achieve your goals according to your business needs and the peculiarity of the local market. We specialize in UK, Europe, China and the rest of Asia. We also collect and process data from various sources to provide insights into market trends, customer behavior and operational efficiency.

Go to market

If you want to enter a new market we can support throughout the whole process: from the very initial stages related to market analysis to the set up of the company/office till the start of your business.

Project Management

From the launch of a pop up store, a store opening/closure, to any specific need a company might have, we oversee each project, ensuring it is completely efficient, on time and within budget. These service include planning, organizing, coordinating and controlling tasks related to any retail/wholesale focused initiative. Interim Management

We cover temporary executive or managerial position at corporate level as well as in store, allowing the business to benefit quickly from the expertise and guidance of a high skilled professional. We intervene in presence starting from 1 day a week up to full time for periods from 1 to 12 months. The service is extremely interesting to achieve defined objectives, stabilize operations, or steer a company through a transition until a permanent solution or a management team is established. It’s the perfect solution for companies facing unexpected changes, lacking of a local structure or requiring specific skills for a finite period. Some of the roles that we can cover: Country Manager, Retail Director/Manager, Retail Project Manager, Business Developer, HR Manager, CRM, Retail Marketing Manager, Store Manager/Director.

Reorganization and recruiting

We help you to optimize and improve the efficiency of your corporate and in store team structure. Once agreed the best solution for your business, we can support you during the local recruiting process of the team, thanks also to our network of local recruiting experts. We can also support you to recruit and supervise the team during pop up or other short term projects.
