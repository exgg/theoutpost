Retail performances and distribution


Retail Management

This service includes performance audits of the point of sale or an entire network of stores to improve their profitability. At the same time looking at the functionality of a corporate office.

It’s fundamnetal to oversee closely the day-to-day operations of a retail establishment for those companies which don’t have a management team in the local market. We offer services at international level to allow for smooth functioning and success. Services includes managing staff, inventory, customer service, sales, merchandising and store operations.

Business development and location search

We can help you to start or enlarge your retail/distribution network in city centers, shopping malls, department stores, outlets and airports, with a special focus on Europe and Asia. Supporting you throughout all phases: opening plan, location research, negotiations with properties/management companies/wholesalers, opening schedules, and supervision of the set-up.

Design and realization of new store concepts, pop ups and installations

Studies of the variuos formats according to your needs to provide the best shopping experience and brand image. This service inculdes project design, coordination and the construction of stores, kiosks, corners, shop in shops, exhibitions and pop up installations.
