📘 ANOTHER BOOK

Blinded by Science

In his ground-breaking book, Blinded by Science, Silverstone uncovers new evidence that the world around us may be having a damaging effect on our health. But with greater understanding of nature’s behavior, we can begin to limit those effects.

Trees, plants, water, the sun, and the moon — all influence how we feel daily. Western science has often ignored thousands of studies suggesting there’s more to our body’s behavior than current biology explains.

By linking the principle that everything vibrates with water’s ability to retain vibrations, Silverstone explores phenomena like mobile phone effects, sleep disorders, homeopathy, and lunar cycles — all in a scientifically grounded, revolutionary way.
